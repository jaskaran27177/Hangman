RULES OF THE GAME:

Play individually

Have the user select a letter of the alphabet.

If the guessed letter is contained in the word, the individual will see the letter displayed in every position of the word the letter occurs.

If the guessed letter is not contained in the word, the individual will see the incorrect guessed label be increased by 1

The total number of incorrect guesses are 7, any more after that will result in a loss for the user

The game continues until:

the word is guessed (all letters are revealed) – WIN or,

more than 7 incorrect guesses are guessed – LOSE
